Who are we?

AVEM-Ed is an African Community that focuses on employing Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics(STEM) education as an empowering tool for Africa's youth. As the world's largest young population, it is critical that the youth are prepared with the required skills to lead the change for growth and development of the continent.
